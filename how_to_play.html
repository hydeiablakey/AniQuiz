How Do I Play?

Step 1: Select One Category between Anime, Manga, or Character Quotes

Step 2: Choose between Four Possible Answer Choices

Step 3: If the Answer Selected is Incorrect, the Answer Will Turn Red and A Screen Will Pop Up Showing the Correct Answer

Step 4: If the Answer Selected is Correct, the Answer Will Turn Green and A Screen Will Pop Up Congratulating You

Step 5: Exit the Pop Up Screen

Step 6: Click on the Next Question Button to Continue

Step 7: Repeat Steps Two Through Six Until Answering Question Ten

Step 8: The Game Will End
